Are You TOPS Eligible?

TOPS (Taylor Opportunity Program for Students) covers tuition at qualifying Louisiana schools. ACT score is one of the key requirements. Every point going into senior year matters.

  • TOPS Tech — 2.5 GPA + 17 ACT
  • TOPS Opportunity — 2.5 GPA + 20 ACT (most common target)
  • TOPS Performance — 3.0 GPA + 23 ACT
  • TOPS Honors — 3.5 GPA + 27 ACT

More info: osfa.la.gov/tops.html

Not yet TOPS eligible? Reach out to Ms. Chasity to sign up for the June ACT. Contact her at chasity@thebryc.org or (303) 817-5835.
